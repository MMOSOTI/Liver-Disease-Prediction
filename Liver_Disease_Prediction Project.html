5. Conclusion¶

The model accuracy of the Decision tree is the highest at 86.9% and therefore can be best used to predict the Liver disease.The results show that age,Gender,BMI, AlcoholConsumption,smoking,GeneneticRisk,diabetes,Hypertension,Liverfunction Test are positively correlated with the Liver Diagnosis.AlcoholConsumption, ,smoking and Gender are the major predictorsof Liver Disease Diagnosis.The results further show that physical activityis negatively related to Liver Diagnosis.
